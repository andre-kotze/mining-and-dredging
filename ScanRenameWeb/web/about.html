ABOUT IMDH SCAN RENAME

IMDH SCAN RENAME reads and renames scanned files using the QR codes located on Geological Screen Logs and Diamond Sorting Logs.
Only .jpg scans are currently supported. Scan documents at 300 dpi. Errors may be encountered with resolutions other than 2480 x 3507.
For support and development needs please get in touch with AKO or FDT. Feedback and suggestions are welcome.
